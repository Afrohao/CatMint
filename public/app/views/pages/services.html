Walk the cat

Need a helper to walk your cat? We'd love to help you! We provide cat harnesses with various materials, which can make your cat comfortable. Also, we'll walk your cat in a safe place to prevent fighting from dogs and cats. Moreover, we'll offer small snacks to your cat every walk with no charge.

Feed the cat

If you have a vacation, we are here to feed your cat. We provide excellent cat food to your cat. Also, if you have special request, please let know and we will do our best.

To the hospital

If you go to a vacation, and your cat need to see a doctor, we are here to help you. We'll go to your home and pick up your cat. We'll send to the designated hospital and send the doctor's message to you. Please call us to ask for more information!
